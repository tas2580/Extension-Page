Instalation

To install this extension download it from here and upload the files in your forum under /ext/{{ext.github}}. After that go to the Admin panel of your forum and navigate in to Customise -> Extension Management -> Extensions. Search for {{ext.name}} in the list of extensions and click on Enable.

Support

You can get support for this extension at phpbb.com or in german language at phpbb.de. For more informations look at my Website.
